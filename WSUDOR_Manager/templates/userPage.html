Welcome to Ouroboros, {{user.displayName}}!

Ouroboros is the primary internal, graphical management tool for the Wayne State University Digital Object Repository (WSUDOR), which supports the Digital Collections, and other initiatives. This graphical interface was designed as an internal application, and had evolved through a handful of iterations. It has stretched and warped to accomodate new digital object types, platform stakeholders, and demands of security and access. Which is all to say, thanks for your patience and pardon the mess.
