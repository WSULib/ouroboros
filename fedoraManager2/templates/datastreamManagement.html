Datastream Management

Datastreams are the granular level of Fedora objects where the actual images, audio files, documents, and metadata files reside. Every object in Fedora has a number of datastreams, including, but cetainly not limited to: RDF relationships, MODS, Dublin Core (DC), images, XACML Policy statements shaping access, or even a single page image in a book.

The utilities below are designed to help the creation, management, and deletion of datastreams in bulk.
